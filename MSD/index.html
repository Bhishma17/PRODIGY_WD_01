About Mahi

M.S. Dhoni (born July 7, 1981, Ranchi, Bihar [now Jharkhand] state, India) is an Indian cricketer whose rise to prominence in the early 21st century culminated in his captaincy of the Indian national team that won the one-day Cricket World Cup in 2011. Dhoni made his international debut in 2004.

Achievements-National Honors

  • Padma Bhushan MS Dhoni was awarded with the Padma Bhushan in 2018, which is India’s third-highest civilian award.
  • Padma Shree In 2009, he also received the Padma Shree, which is India’s fourth-highest civilian award.
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna In 2007-08 where Dhoni was awarded with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, which is the highest honor in India given for achievements in sports.
